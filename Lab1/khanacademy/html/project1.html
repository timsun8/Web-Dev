Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 3/4 cup
Egg 1 large
Butter (melted) 2 tbsp
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Step 1: In a bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

Step 2: In another bowl, mix the wet ingredients: milk, egg, and melted butter. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (donâ€™t overmix).

Step 3: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and grease lightly. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400 g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan Cheese (grated) 1 cup
Pancetta or Bacon 150 g
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Black Pepper 1 tsp

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk eggs and grated Parmesan cheese together.

Step 3: In a skillet, cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.

Step 4: Add hot spaghetti to the skillet and remove from heat. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce. Serve immediately.
